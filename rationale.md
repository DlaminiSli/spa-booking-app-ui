# Design Rationale – BrightSpa Appointment Booking App

## Target Users
The app is designed for:
- Individuals booking spa services
- Busy users who prefer quick mobile booking
- First-time users unfamiliar with booking systems

The design focuses on simplicity and ease of use.

---

## Booking Flow Logic
The process is step-by-step:
1. Login or sign up
2. Select service
3. View details
4. Select specialist
5. Choose date and time
6. Review summary
7. Confirm booking

---

## UX Decisions
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Step-by-step flow
- Card-based layouts
- Visual service representation
- Specialist selection
- Easy time slot selection
- Booking summary confirmation
- Consistent color theme
- Bottom navigation for mobile

---

## User Experience Improvement
The design improves experience by:
- Reducing booking friction
- Improving clarity
- Building trust
- Enhancing mobile usability
- Providing clear feedback
- Supporting quick decision-making
